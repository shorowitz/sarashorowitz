About Me

I am an enthusiastic and artistic full stack web developer specializing in data visualization and all things Javascript.

I am now a graduate of the Web Development Immersive and the Data Visualization course, and believe that I have found the talent and the passion that I had always been searching for in my professional life.

My skills include but are not limited to:

  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • React.js
  • D3.js
